Top 5 Signature Dishes

Sushi

Sushi is a traditional Japanese dish that typically consists of vinegared rice combined with various ingredients, including seafood, vegetables, and occasionally tropical fruits.

Noodles

Noodles are a staple food in many cultures, particularly in Asian cuisines, and they come in a wide variety of types and preparations. They are typically made from wheat, rice, or starch.

Chunky Soups

Woo bowl!

This recipe, shared by Chef Ronnie Woo, is a comforting dish that combines seasoned ground beef with rice and a soft-cooked egg. It is described as "instant comfort food" and is easy to prepare.
